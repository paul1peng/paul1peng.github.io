About

My name is Paul Peng and I am Paul Peng. I am a drawer currently based in Pittsburgh, where I study art and computer science at Carnegie Mellon University.

My work comes from the tensions between physical and virtual spaces, set in mid-2000s teen power fantasy and the contradiction of the representational drawing. I work creature creation, non-human figures, and fanfiction affect, in competition with the materiality and process of mark-making, to explore ad-hoc remakings of identities and pre-existing worlds into more ideal images.

My work also explores how this remaking process can itself be made into a more ideal one: higher-order fanfictions born from high art contexts and reading too much continental philosophy.

I also study programming language theory for fun.
