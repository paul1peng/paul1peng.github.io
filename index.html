About

Statement

We begin with the teenage power fantasies of the mid-2000s: the internet becomes a platform for us to play our fantasies against one another. No longer must we restrict our double-lives as neon-colored dog-taurs, as demon-angel cyborgs, as glittered wolves and Sonic the Hedgehog fan-characters, to our minds and the margins of our notebooks. Our space is expanded into a shared one, where the metallic claws and heavenly wings of others may at last touch our neon-colored paws.

Space expands at the touch of this neon paw.

I draw from these shared teenage power fantasies into a weird bodily, spatial, performative stew. From their death at the hands of high school art teachers and bad-art blogs into their rebirth in post-irony, in high art contexts, in hyperreality. Furry porn in a post-structuralist phenomenological wrapper.

I draw these reborn power fantasies themselves. Knockoff-anim√© furries, retrofitted with entry-level continental philosophy and Young Thug mixtapes, play in a space born from the intersection of others: representational space, fantasy space, paper space, margin space. These drawings emerge from an initial, central gesture that fixes our space and then plays towards its own unfixing, its own de-centering. Our furry bodies, likewise, play towards the high art contexts that fund their second chance: to expand beyond the spaces that make sense of them.

What does it mean when our neon dog-taurs, our demon-angel cyborgs, our glittered wolves and Sonic the Hedgehog fan-characters expand beyond these spaces? This question is senseless; they were always here, in a thin layer of graphite on deckle-edged 100% cotton paper, taking selfies to show off their latest Hood By Air threads as the sounds of the latest Young Thug mixtape filter in through their brand new iPhones. Meanwhile, the verdant nightscape that wraps them threatens to fall apart into the made marks that implement them, a space that expands and collapses at the same time.

This is how to expand beyond fantasies: to perform as what you want to become to become what you want to become.
